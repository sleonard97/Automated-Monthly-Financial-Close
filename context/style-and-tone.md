# Style and Tone

## Audience

The primary audience is a finance analyst reviewing monthly close results for a charter school.

## AI Review Prompt Style

- Use concise analyst-note language.
- Focus on why the account should be reviewed.
- Avoid cause claims unless the data directly supports them.
- Avoid action directives that imply final financial judgment.
- Do not mention vendor names, customer names, descriptions, or memos.
- Keep notes specific to calculated fields such as actuals, forecast, remaining dollars, percent used, and benchmark.

## Good Note Pattern

`Review because this account has used 83% of the full-year forecast by February, above the 67% straight-line benchmark. Confirm whether remaining forecast is sufficient.`

## Avoid

- `This variance was caused by staffing changes.`
- `Reduce spending immediately.`
- `Vendor ABC drove the overage.`
- `This is definitely an error.`
